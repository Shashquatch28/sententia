# Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team

## About Redrob AI

Redrob AI is a Series A, AI-native talent intelligence platform based in Pune/Noida, India. We are building the next generation of recruitment infrastructure powered by LLMs and RAG pipelines.

## About the Role

We are looking for a Senior AI Engineer to join us as a founding-team hire. You will build LLM and RAG systems that power our core talent intelligence products — from candidate matching to recruiter copilots. This is a high-ownership, high-ambiguity role where your architecture decisions will directly shape the product roadmap.

## What You'll Do

- Design and build production LLM/RAG pipelines for talent intelligence (candidate ranking, skill extraction, fit scoring)
- Own end-to-end AI feature development — from architecture to deployment and monitoring
- Work directly with the founding team to translate business requirements into technical specs
- Evaluate and integrate the latest LLM tooling (LangChain, LlamaIndex, vector databases)
- Build evaluation frameworks to measure AI output quality in production

## What We're Looking For

**Must Have**
- 5–9 years of software engineering experience with at least 2 years in ML/AI systems
- Production experience building LLM or RAG systems (not just prototypes)
- Strong Python skills — FastAPI, async patterns, production-grade code
- Hands-on with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Chroma, pgvector)
- Comfort with model deployment, monitoring, and iteration in production

**Strong Plus**
- Experience fine-tuning or adapting foundation models
- Prior startup or founding-team experience
- Familiarity with the recruitment / HR tech domain
- Active GitHub presence with AI/ML contributions

## Location

Pune or Noida, India (hybrid). Series A compensation + meaningful equity.
