# Senior Product Manager — AI/ML Platform

## About the Role

We are building the infrastructure that powers the next generation of AI products. As Senior PM for our ML Platform, you will own the developer-facing surface of our training and inference stack — the tools, APIs, and workflows that 500+ internal and external ML engineers rely on every day.

This is a high-ownership, high-ambiguity role. You will set the product vision for our ML Platform, ruthlessly prioritise a backlog that spans model training pipelines, experiment tracking, feature stores, and model deployment — and you will do it in partnership with four engineering teams who ship fast and expect you to keep up.

## What You'll Do

- Define and own the roadmap for our ML Platform developer experience: from dataset versioning to model serving APIs
- Partner directly with ML engineers, data scientists, and infrastructure teams to understand pain points and translate them into clear, well-scoped PRDs
- Lead cross-functional alignment across platform engineering, applied ML, and external enterprise customers
- Drive data-informed product decisions: define success metrics, design experiments, and own the dashboards that tell you whether you're winning
- Be the external voice of the platform: lead customer discovery calls, evaluate competitive landscape, and translate market signals into strategy
- Represent product in technical design reviews — you don't need to write the code, but you need to understand the tradeoffs

## What We're Looking For

**Must Have**
- 4–9 years of product management experience with at least 2 years owning a technical or platform product
- Track record of shipping products that engineers and/or data scientists actually use and love
- Strong technical intuition — comfortable in a design review, fluent in API design discussions, able to write a SQL query to pull your own data
- Experience working cross-functionally in fast-paced, high-ambiguity environments
- Demonstrated ownership mindset: you've shipped things end-to-end, you've made hard prioritisation calls, you have data to back them up

**Strong Plus**
- Prior experience with ML infrastructure, MLOps, model training pipelines, or AI developer tooling
- Familiarity with LLMs, foundation models, or the modern AI stack
- Experience at a Series B–D company where you had to build process as you went
- Worked at a company where engineering is the primary customer

## Our Values

We move fast but not carelessly. We believe in first-principles thinking, strong opinions loosely held, and a bias for action. We ship, we learn, we iterate. Ownership means you care about outcomes, not just outputs — we don't celebrate activity, we celebrate impact.
